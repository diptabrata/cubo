About us

Cubbo bookings

Whether it’s by bringing our artists to your favourite clubs and festivals; producing videosets and aftermovies for you to enjoy after the party; or even by sharing with you exclusive sets from international artists with our weekly podcast, Cubbo is becoming a reference for the lovers of electronic music around the world.

With our headquarters and a strong presence in Barcelona, Spain, our agency has the pleasure to count with some of the most renowned artists of the techno scene, as well as new talents which we believe will be shaping the scene in the future.

Our aim is to forge and consolidate these musical genres and create a solid electronic music culture around our artists by supporting and promoting the with the experience and the knowledge that we have of this industry.
