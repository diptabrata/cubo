Shitake

Shitake

BIOGRAPHY

_

Barcelona, Spain

_

One of the most prolific and talented artists in the world

It was the year 1989 when a young Shitake with only 11 years began.
When the boys dream of videogames and go outside to play, Manresano Carles Pérez aka Shitake dreamed of owning his own Technics. Since then Shitake has only had one thing on his head: vinyl spinning on plates.

Very soon he began training at home and in different places of friends, at 18 he already played in front of the public. Two years later he was already a resident of a club where he would share a booth with artists of the level of Terry Francis, Jim Masters, Stacey Pullen, Ben Sims, among others ... after several years in several groups in the area, he joined the prestigious promoter HAPPY TECHNO who, with her residence in City Hall at parties with the same name, sharing a booth with artists such as Marshall Jefferson, Oliver Hunteman, Joe Rundell, Dj Murphy or Cristian Varela, then the absolute dominance of the Barcelona scene and the European scene performing in countries such as Holland, Germany, Belgium, France, etc ... But Shirake not only clicks, also produces with Lexlay for one of the most important labels of the scene, Happy Techno Music, taking out His first references with a very personal and intimate touch, recently signed by Cubbo Bookings, the future of this charismatic DJ is undoubtedly something very promising, what will be the following?
