Complexed Records label chief heads out with another arresting production entitled Hear My Sound, a hard-hitting release that will light up just about any dancefloor it’s unleashed on. Functional yet catchy, it encapsulates the German producer’s main ethos by showcasing a straightforward, spellbinding groove accented with a powerful vocal loop.
Splattered with bleeps and fizzing synths, it keeps its dynamic factor on both tracks, whether the mood calls for a vocal or simply its robust, engrossing instrumental.
