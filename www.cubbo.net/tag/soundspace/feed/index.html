Besides being known as the leading voice behind AudioCode Records and Devotion Records, Brazil-born, Spain-based DJ and producer Fernanda Martins has collaborated with names like Matt Minimal and Spartaque and put music out on Loose Records and Nothing But.

For the 214th edition of our mix series, Fernanda includes an impressive 17 tracks. The sounds of which include Cari Lekebusch, INSOLATE, Moddular, Bas Mooy, Dino Maggiorana and more. You can find the full mix and tracklist below.
