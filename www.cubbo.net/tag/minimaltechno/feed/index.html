At the beginning of a friendship between Mr. Bizz , Jannowitz and BOHO. We decided to release the two Italians even more on JAW. After they already released on Cocoon, Terminal M, Sci+tec and co… Mr.Bizz now start with their “Rush” Ep. We invite and unite Italy & Spain with: Lorenzo Bartoletti & SILES as remixers.Because the two of them already released on the favorite labels of BOHO and so they deliver quality music! Besides, there are currently very few producers of groovy and pumping sounds like these two masterminds. We can not wait to release more of them in the future! And please pay attention to the incredible artwork of the world start tattoo artist Paul Talbot Karma Punk.
