It’s such a tradition to have Caal & Baum music on the label for the summer season. After ‘Downtown Beat’ and ‘Rockin Shoes’, this usual suspects delivered two housy weapons ready for a wide range of Djs and clubbers. It’s time to ‘Funk Dat’ and ‘Trycan’.

The lead track of this release, ‘Funk Dat’, is a vocal track featuring Menesix, a talent-ed artist from Amsterdam, whom transform an old 90s vocal in something up-to-date with a lot of potential. Heavy low-end and funny beat for all audiences. ‘Trycan’ is the other option in this EP, 2000s house feeling with modern production, a proven and tested dancefloor bomb. The guys did it again!
