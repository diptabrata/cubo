Issa Elle – ‘MAMBALE’ EP https://www.cubbo.net/davina-moss-feat-issa-elle-mambale-ep/ https://www.cubbo.net/davina-moss-feat-issa-elle-mambale-ep/#respond Sun, 24 Feb 2019 18:08:28 +0000 http://stage.cubbo.net/?p=1631 We’re all about the debuts this year on ADOBE , next up we’re proud to present the latest EP from French DJ and producer, Davina Moss featuring the vocals of the very talented Issa Elle. Davina has made a name for herself with a DJ career spanning over 15 years with accomplished releases on respectable labels; Hot Creations, ViVa, Do Not Sleep and Emerald City and her tracks have gained support from some of the biggest names in the industry including Jamie Jones, Richy Ahmed, Detlef and Patrick Topping. Title track ‘Mambale’ reflects Davina’s signature swinging drums and a groove laden bassline with Issa Elle’s sultry vocals giving the track a tribal feel. Completing the EP is Piero Pirupa’s remix which offers a slightly techier take on the original, with rolling percussion and synth builds making it the perfect club workout.
