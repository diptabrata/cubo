Baum has been spending more and more time in the studio lately. After releasing on Sola, Repopulate Mars, Abode or elrow Music, he will debut with his latest release “Block Party EP” on one of the coolest US electronic music labels – Desert Hearts.
Check out Baum’s two up-to-date tech-house for the dance floor collabs with his partner in crime Caal and an awesome remix by Marco Strous.
