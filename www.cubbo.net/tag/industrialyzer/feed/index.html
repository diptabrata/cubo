The japanese techno master Ken Ishii makes his debut in Code Records with an excellent song called ?Escape?. The track has an hypnotic synth travelling up and down over a killer base that gives an incredible groove to the whole set. The release also includes 2 amazing remixes from Industrialyzer, both of them with its own personality and both of them deserving to be considered huge floorfillers. The EP is completed with another remix by Nuke, one of the owners of the label, that gives his own view of the original version, experimenting with the groove and the synth and giving a preview of what we can expect from his upcoming releases in Phobiq and Unity Records.
