]]> Juli Aristy joins Cubbo https://www.cubbo.net/juli-aristy-joins-cubbo/ Tue, 06 Aug 2019 08:01:00 +0000 https://www.cubbo.net/?p=5827 We are glad to announce the newest member of the CUBBO artist pool.

Dj And Producer Juli Aristy Is Considered To Be One Of Most Important Colombian Techno Exports Who Is Currently Based Out Of Europe. Juli Has Been Djing Since 2008 And She Played Some Of The Most Important Festivals And Clubs Around South America And Europe In Countries Like: Mexico, Panamá, Ecuador, Perú, Guatemala, Venezuela, Aruba, Colombia, USA, Netherlands, Spain, Croatia, Serbia, Poland And More.

Juli Aristy Was Chosen As Best Colombian DJ By Pioneer DJ Contest In 2013 And Best Colombian Techno DJ By CDA Awards In 2017. Her Perfomances Are Energetic And Have The Power To Exceed Expectations For Many. Juli Always Makes Sure To Have A Strong Connection With Her Fans And Audience Taking Them On A Musical Journey Through Sets That Are Full Of Hypnotic Beats And Fearless Mixing Behind The Decks.

As A Producer, Juli Has Been Working In The Studio For Years Developing Her Own Sound, With More Than 50 Digital And Vinyl Releases Worldwide Featured On Labels Such As Unity Records Or Transmit Recordings. 2019 Has Been A Defining Year For Juli Due To Many Of Her Productions Reaching The Top 100 Techno Chart On Beatport Alongside Names Like D-Unity And Spartaque.

Entering The Beatport Top 100 Charts With Every Release In 2019 Was Just The Beginning And She Continues Being Supported By Artists Such Joseph Capriati, Richie Hawtin, Marco Carola, Charlotte De Witte, SinisaTamamovic, Markantonio, Spartaqle, Umek, Ramiro Lopez, Luigi Madonna, Hollen, Spektre, Steve Mulder, D-Unity, And Many More.

There Is Absolutely No Doubt Why She Is Considered One Of The Best South American Techno Acts With Current Biggest International Projection.

More Than 10 Years Of Dedicated Hard Work Both Behind The Decks And The Studio Has Made Her Reach New Heights Of Her Career With No End Of What Is About To Come Next!

Enjoy her latest album Follow The Girl.
