Mindshake is back in September with a properly paired Various Artists compilation to celebrate the label’s storied relationship with Amsterdam Dance Event.

After playing an intrical role in ADE’s Dockyard Festival event for many years running with Mindshake’s notorious Label Showcase, Paco Osuna brings his label’s first Various Artists release in close to 6 years right to your ears with a slew of Originals from characters who have been featured on many of the Showcase’s bills, as well as other Mindshake Showcases throughout the globe.

From the masterful and unique techno of Egbert, to the floor-stomping, hypnotic and head-swirling techno of Eats Everything, Avision, and Sinisa Tamamovic… to the label’s anticipated collaboration between Andres Campo & Fer BR… to the near-perfect techno tinged follow-up to his recent Mindshake EP ala Ariel Rodz… to the minimal techno leanings of Colombian veteran Sean Random and the peak hour tech house return of the prodigal American Dela (USA)… this VA absolutely has it ALL.
