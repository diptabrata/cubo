LABEL Koukou Records

CATALOG KKU029

Grab a copy:
https://www.beatport.com/release/double-trouble/2647661

Kuukou Records new EP is a techno focused release from label boss Simina Grigoriu with remixes by Alberto Ruiz and AlBird.

Alberto Ruiz has close links to Pig&Dan having released on their label Elevate and collaborated on music with them. He has also featured on many other labels such as Suara, Transmit, Relief and his own Stickrecordings, amongst lots more of a similar calibre.

Rounding off the artists on the release is AlBird, who has previously showcased his talents on imprints such as Set About and BluFin.

The original cut “Double Trouble” has pulsating synths and thrashing hi-hats at the heart of its peak time groove that has just a hint of euphoria. Alberto Ruiz builds on the energy with the addition of some fast-flowing percussion, and his own lead synth brings a sinister vibe to the track. Finishing off the tracks here is the acid-tinged and explosive remix from AlBird.

LABEL Voltage Records

CATALOG VLT031

Grab a copy:
https://www.beatport.com/release/time-code/2643998

Alberto Ruiz returns to VOLTAGE with a blasting 2 tracker!

Percussive,, powerful and intense, like most of the Spaniard’s work, Time Code EP is a combination of two great compositions manifested with Alberto’s sheer talent and love for his work.

What better way to celebrate this release other than with two very unique remixes?

Legendary German producer Jam El Mar from of Jam & Spoon duo, a creator of iconic landmark music like “Right in the Night” and “Stella”, is one of the artists that forged generations and inspired thousands of producers worldwide. Making us an incredible honour to join the warm family of our label, he has remixed Alberto’s original “Tentaculo”.

But this release is far from over, because the second remix on the same original has been created by the master of solid techno, Sven Wittekind himself. His approach is spot on, combining the melodic elements of the original with the depth and power of his own unique style making this EP a solid weapon!
