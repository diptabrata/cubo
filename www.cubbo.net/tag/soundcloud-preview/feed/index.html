Metaphase is coming to its 9th release by the imprint-running duo, Steam Shape, with a strong four-tracker EP.

The EP is going with a balance in my mind, where two originals are meant to hammer the peak-time hours, while the other two are bringing more melodic elements to this single’s diversity. As it was experienced before, the tracks have their unique Steam Shape sound with rock-steady drum section, heavy bass foundation, answering percussion elements and hypnotizing synth stabs in order to serve as an ultimate tool in one’s portfolio.
